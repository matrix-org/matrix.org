As announced previously, The Matrix Conference is set to take place in Malmö, Sweden, in October 2026.

While the Call for Proposals [is still open](https://cfp.2026.matrix.org/matrix-conference-2026/cfp) until the end of June, we are happy to announce that you can [buy a ticket](https://conference.matrix.org/register/) for the Conference right now!

<!-- more -->

To ensure you get the best deal, don’t wait until the last minute to register. You have until the **end of May** to secure an Early Bird ticket and save up to 20% on the ticket price. This is also your chance to pre-order official merch and secure the right size for you. We will also sell merch at the Conference itself, with no guarantees that we will get your size in stock.

We also have a special opportunity for those who want to contribute their time to make the event a success. Volunteers who enroll for two shifts or more on our [volunteering platform](https://volunteer.matrix.org/) will get a free ticket and a special-edition zipper hoodie.

![A blue zipper hoodie as seen from the back. It has a logo of The Matrix Conference 2026 embroidered in yellow.](/blog/img/2026-05-04-conference-volunteer-hoodie.avif)

We’re looking forward to seeing you in Malmö!
