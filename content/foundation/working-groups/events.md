## `Meetups & Conferences` Space

The Meetups & Conferences Space is maintained by the Events WG as a place for community members to find real life events about Matrix and connect to others in the Matrix ecosystem.

The space contains rooms for various local meetups, rooms for Matrix meetups / groups at other events, as well as Matrix Community Events like the Matrix Community Summit or events organized by The Matrix Foundation such as The Matrix Conference.

If you have an event you're organizing and want listed in the space, you should know the following:

### Policy

- The room is on topic, i.e. it officially represents an event which is concerned with Matrix itself as topic, or unofficially, however clearly stating so.
- We only add one room or space per event.
- The room is actively moderated:
  - It ideally has more than 1 human moderator.
  - Spam is removed within reasonable time. Automated tooling should be used, please reach out if you have questions about that.
  - Moderators are responsive to the WG and Foundation staff's requests.
- The room admins/owners agree to have the room listed in the space.

### Process & Decision making

Please reach out to the WG via its Matrix room [#events-wg:matrix.org](https://matrix.to/#events-wg:matrix.org) or privately by email to [events-wg@foundation.matrix.org](mailto:events-wg@foundation.matrix.org).
The Events WG will check against the policy and notify you about the decision to include it or not.
WG decisions may be overruled by the Foundation's Trust & Safety team.
