A simple Android SMS (text message) <--> Matrix bridge. With this bridge you can get all your text messages on Matrix and reply to them.

Get it and report issues at [github](https://github.com/tijder/SmsMatrix), or [install from F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/eu.droogers.smsmatrix/).
