An Android app that wraps [mautrix-imessage](https://github.com/tulir/mautrix-imessage)
to bridge SMS from your Android phone to Matrix.

Setup instructions [on docs.mau.fi](https://docs.mau.fi/bridges/go/imessage/android/setup.html).

✓ SMS (text messages)  
✓ MMS (photos and other attachments)  
✗ RCS  
✓ Group chats  
✓ Backfilling messages from Android SMS database  
✓ Double puppeting (for backfilling)  
✓ End-to-bridge encryption
