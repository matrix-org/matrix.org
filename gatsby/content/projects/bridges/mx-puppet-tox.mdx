[mx-puppet-tox](https://github.com/Sorunome/mx-puppet-tox) bridges tox and matrix together via (double)puppeting. It basically acts as a tox client for matrix.

&#10003; Direct (1:1) Chats  
&#10007; Group Chats  
&#10003; Plain Text  
&#10007; Formatted Messages (unsupported by tox)  
&#10003; Media/files  
&#10003; Presence (Tox &#8594; Matrix only)  
&#10003; Typing notifications (Tox &#8594; Matrix only)  
&#10003; Multi-user  

This bridge is part of the [mx-puppet-bridge](https://github.com/Sorunome/mx-puppet-bridge) suite of puppeting bridges.
