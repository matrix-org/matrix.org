[Matrix Knowledge Base](https://gitlab.com/Matrixcoffee/matrix-knowledge-base) is a community-supported collection of FAQs and more on all things [Matrix](https://matrix.org). Its primary purpose is to serve as botfood for [FAQBot][], but it is also suitable for direct human consumption.

To add your own knowledge to [Matrix Knowledge Base](https://gitlab.com/Matrixcoffee/matrix-knowledge-base) and to [FAQBot][], [open an issue](https://gitlab.com/Matrixcoffee/matrix-knowledge-base/issues/new) or [submit a merge request](https://gitlab.com/Matrixcoffee/matrix-knowledge-base/compare).

[FAQBot]: https://gitlab.com/Matrixcoffee/FAQBot
