How to use them

When reproducing the Matrix logo, please include an appropriate disclaimer and link back to matrix.org, for example

This logo is the trademark of The Matrix.org Foundation. Use of this logo does not imply endorsement or affiliation.

The Foundation's trademark policy allows you to create derivative works using the above logos within the defined limits.

  • Please do not skew or alter the aspect ratio of the logos.
  • The official colors are #000000 Black and #FFFFFF White.
  • The font is based on Helvetica Neue bold, which you can easily use to create visually fitting variations of the logo.
